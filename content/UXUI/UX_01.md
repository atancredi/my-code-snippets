

# Elements of User Experience
1. Strategy: what value are we providing?
2. Scope: what are we building?
3. Structure: how is it organized?
4. Skeleton: what signifiers are needed?
5. Surface: what style should it use?

# Short form creative brief
1. Project objective
2. Key personas

    list one or two personas they were designing this particular functionality for
3. Key scenarios

    describe up to three short scenarios that the design was trying to solve
4. Key principles

    come up with several guiding principles

[source](https://articles.centercentre.com/short_form_creative_brief/)


# Value Creation
- Useful

    does your product actually solves a real problem.?
    
- Usable

    can people actually operate your product without pulling their hair out?

- Findable

    determines whether users can locate what they need when they need it.

- Credible

    establishes whether users trust your product and your organization.

- Accessible

    ensures your product works for people with different abilities and in different contexts.

- Desirable

    does people actually want to engage with your products?
